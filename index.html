Dono Games

How to play

Each round compares two universities. One rank is visible; your job is to decide whether the hidden university is closer to #1 or further away.

#12Read the known rank#1 is the highest rank, so smaller numbers are better.

↑Choose HigherUse this when you think it is closer to #1.
↓Choose LowerUse this when you think it is further from #1.

Scoring: Three Lives and Daily Challenge end after three incorrect answers. Timed gives you 60 seconds to score as many points as you can.
